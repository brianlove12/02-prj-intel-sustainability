SUSTAINABILITY THROUGH THE AGES

Explore Intel's journey through time, discovering how our commitment to innovation has shaped a more sustainable future for technology and our planet.

Intel's Sustainability Timeline

1968

Intel Founded

Intel Corporation is founded by Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore, marking the beginning of decades of innovation.

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel introduces the 4004, the world's first commercial microprocessor, launching the microprocessor revolution.

1978

8086 Processor

Intel launches the 8086 processor, establishing the x86 architecture that powers countless computers today.

1985

386 Microprocessor

Intel introduces the 386 microprocessor, bringing 32-bit computing and multitasking to personal computers.

2022

100% Renewable Energy

Intel achieved 100% renewable electricity use in its global operations.

2030

Net Positive Water

Intel aims to achieve net positive water use by conserving more water than it consumes.

2040

Net Zero Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Intel is committed to reaching net zero greenhouse gas emissions in its global operations.
